Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Here's a time line of Dr.Borlaug's life:

  • 1913- Born in Cresco,Iowa
  • 1933- Leaves his family's farm to attend the University of Minnesota, thanks to a Depression era program known as the "National Youth Administration"
  • 1935- Has to stop school and save up more money. Works in the Civilian Conservation Corps, helping starving Americans. "I saw how food changed them", he said. "All of this leftscars on me."
